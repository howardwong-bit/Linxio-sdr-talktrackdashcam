Golden rules

  • 🎯
    Lead with the problem, not the product.
    Never open with "we have a dashcam." Open with the gap — what they can't see.
  • 👂
    Listen more than you talk.
    If the customer is talking, you are winning. Do not interrupt.
  • 🔥
    You are warming, not closing.
    Howard closes. Your job is to create curiosity and book the handoff.
  • 📋
    Log every call.
    No call goes unlogged. Even a not-interested is data.
  • 🚫
    Never quote a price.
    Pricing is Howard's conversation. Deflect every single time.
